# Privacy Policy — DareFox

**Effective Date:** February 18, 2026  
**Developer:** Marc Vallverdu

## Overview

DareFox is a daily courage challenge app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: none) and how we handle it.

---

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

DareFox does not:
- Collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information
- Require you to create an account or log in
- Track your location
- Use analytics or crash reporting tools
- Share any data with third parties
- Connect to any server or backend service

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

All app data (your progress, streaks, completed dares, and fox companion state) is stored **locally on your device** using your device's built-in storage (AsyncStorage). This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or anyone else
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

---

## Notifications

DareFox uses **local notifications only** — reminders are scheduled directly on your device. We do not use push notification servers, and no data is transmitted to send you reminders. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

---

## Third-Party Services

DareFox does **not** use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data. There are no advertising networks, analytics platforms, or tracking tools of any kind.

---

## Children's Privacy

DareFox is designed for all ages and is rated 4+. Since we collect no personal information from anyone, the app is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide. We do not knowingly (or unknowingly) collect data from children or adults.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this document. Since we collect no data, any changes would only be to clarify our existing practices.
